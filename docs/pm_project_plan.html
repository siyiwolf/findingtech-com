4. Risk Register & Mitigation

Risk Probability Impact Mitigation Strategy
Manufacturing delays Medium High - Dual sourcing strategy
- Build buffer time (2 weeks)
- Weekly factory check-ins
Cost overruns Medium High - 20% contingency budget
- Target cost negotiations
- Quarterly cost reviews
Quality issues Low High - Third-party QC at factory
- Pilot batch approval required
- Clear quality specs in contract
Supply chain disruption Low Medium - Identify backup suppliers
- Safety stock for critical components
Website downtime Medium Medium - Load testing pre-launch
- CDN & backup hosting
- Monitoring & alerting
Poor market reception Medium High - Pre-launch signups target 500+
- Beta customer testimonials
- A/B test landing pages
IP infringement Low Medium - Trademark search before branding
- NDA with all suppliers
- Patent search for design
Payment gateway issues Low Medium - Secondary gateway backup
- Test transactions pre-launch
Team burnout Medium Medium - Sustainable pace (40-45h/wk)
- Clear weekend boundaries
- Cross-training for redundancy
Competitor launch conflict Low Medium - Monitor competitor activity
- Differentiation in branding
- Flexibility in timing (±1 week)

5. Meeting Cadence

Daily Standups (15 min)

  • When: Every weekday, 9:30 AM
  • Who: Core team (all internal)
  • Format:
    1. What did you complete yesterday?
    2. What will you work on today?
    3. Any blockers?
  • Tool: Quick text update in team chat if async preferred
  • Goal: Alignment, early problem detection

Weekly Sync (60 min)

  • When: Mondays, 10:00 AM
  • Who: Full team + key external partners (invited as needed)
  • Agenda:
    1. Wins & highlights from last week (5 min)
    2. Progress against milestones (15 min)
    3. Risk review & mitigation (10 min)
    4. Upcoming week’s priorities (15 min)
    5. Cross-functional dependencies (10 min)
    6. Q&A / decisions needed (5 min)
  • Deliverable: Meeting notes + updated project tracker

Milestone Reviews (90 min)

  • When: At end of each phase (Weeks 6, 9, 12)
  • Who: Leadership + core team
  • Purpose: Phase completion, go/no-go decisions, lessons learned
  • Deliverable: Phase report + recommendations

Ad-hoc Meetings

  • As needed for problem-solving, design reviews, vendor negotiations
  • Schedule 30-min blocks with specific agenda
  • Avoid recurring meetings that aren’t delivering value

6. Success Metrics

Launch Week Targets: - 100+ orders - $25,000+ GMV - <2% site down time - <24h average fulfillment time - NPS ≥ 40 (early customers)

Month 1: - 500 orders - 10% conversion rate - <5% return rate - Media coverage in 3+ outlets
