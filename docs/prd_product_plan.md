## 1. Product Categories

We will launch with **7 core categories** covering the car camping and accessible glamping ecosystems:

1. **Shelters** - Tents, canopies, tarps
2. **Sleep Systems** - Sleeping bags, pads, pillows, air mattresses
3. **Backpacks & Bags** - Daypacks, expedition packs, duffels, storage
4. **Cookware & Kitchen** - Stoves, cook sets, utensils, coolers, tableware
5. **Furniture & Comfort** - Camp chairs, tables, hammocks, lighting
6. **Accessories** - Stakes, footprints, gear repair, organization
7. **Consumables** - Fuel, water filters, batteries (via replenishment model)

Each category will have 3 quality tiers: **Economy**, **Mid-Range**, and **Premium**.

## 2. Category Details & Specifications

### 2.1 Shelters (Tents)

**Economy Tier** ($79-199)
- Capacity: 2-6 person
- Materials: Polyester canopy, fiberglass poles, basic zippers
- Waterproof rating: 1500mm HH (hydrostatic head)
- Weight: 8-15 lbs
- Features: Simple setup, basic ventilation, 1-2 doors
- Target: First-time campers, budget families
- SKUs: 3 sizes (2P, 4P, 6P) × 2 colors = 6

**Mid-Range Tier** ($200-399)
- Capacity: 2-8 person
- Materials: Ripstop nylon, aluminum poles, #5 YKK zippers
- Waterproof rating: 2500-3000mm HH, taped seams
- Weight: 5-10 lbs
- Features: Tool-free setup, multiple vents, interior pockets, gear loft, modular footprint
- Target: Weekend Warriors, regular car campers
- SKUs: 4 sizes (2P, 4P, 6P, 8P) × 3 colors = 12

**Premium Tier** ($400-899)
- Capacity: 2-6 person
- Materials: 20D-30D silicone-treated nylon, DAC aluminum poles, water-resistant #5 zippers
- Waterproof rating: 3000+mm HH, fully taped, bathtub floor
- Weight: 3-6 lbs (ultralight principles)
- Features: Quick-pitch system, 2-3 doors, vertical vents, integrated LED lighting, premium carry bag, modular interior (dividers)
- Target: Glamping enthusiasts, serious campers
- SKUs: 2 sizes (4P, 6P) × 3 colors = 6

**Total Shelter SKUs:** 24

**Differentiation:**
- All tents feature our signature minimalist Scandinavian design
- Neutral color palette (Slate, Olive, Sand) with accent options
- Cross-category color coordination (same colors across furniture, sleep systems)
- Ultralight construction borrowed from backpacking tech for car camping (20-30% weight reduction vs. competitors)
- Lifetime warranty on structural components; 5-year on fabric

## 3. Sourcing Strategy

### 3.1 Manufacturing Regions

We will adopt a **dual-sourcing strategy** to balance cost, quality, and risk:

**Primary: China (70% of volume)**
- **Regions:** Zhejiang (hardware, tents), Fujian (textiles), Guangdong (electronics/lighting)
- **Why:** Mature supply chain, economies of scale, skilled labor, lower MOQs possible for mixed shipments
- **Partners:**
  - Tent & shelters: Specialized OEMs with decades of experience, ISO 9001 certified, previous export to US/EU
  - Textiles: Factories with bluesign® or Oeko-Tex certifications
  - Metalwork: Aluminum anodizing capabilities, CNC machining for precision components

**Secondary: Vietnam (20% of volume)**
- **Regions:** Ho Chi Minh City, Hanoi industrial zones
- **Why:** Tariff advantages for US (if needed), "China+1" diversification, competitive labor costs, strong in textiles and wood/furniture
- **Partners:** Factories with experience in outdoor equipment, BSCI/SA8000 compliance

**Tertiary: Nearshore (10% of volume for speed/agility)**
- **Mexico:** For fast-turnaround items, emergencies, or USMCA tariff benefits
- **Bangladesh:** For basic textiles and accessories (cost-driven)
- **India:** For hardware and metal components

### 3.2 Supplier Criteria

**Mandatory Requirements:**
- ISO 9001 or equivalent quality management
- BSCI, SA8000, or similar social compliance certification
- No child labor, safe working conditions, fair wages
- Environmental compliance: wastewater treatment, emissions controls
- Willingness to sign NDA and IP protection agreement
- English-speaking representative
- Experience exporting to US/EU (with references)

**Preferred Qualifications:**
- Previous work with outdoor brands (e.g., supplied to REI co-op brands, decathlon, etc.)
- Ability to handle mixed SKU orders (not huge single-SKU runs)
- Small-batch production capability (for testing and agility)
- In-house tooling and mold-making
- CBAM or carbon footprint reporting capability
- bluesign®, Oeko-Tex, GOTS certifications for textiles
- ISO 14001 environmental management

**Quality Control:**
- Pre-production samples approved by our team
- In-process inspections at 30%, 70%, and 100% completion
- Third-party QC agency (SGS, BV, Intertek) for final inspection
- AQL 1.0 for major defects, 2.5 for minor defects
- We will send our own QC representatives for first 3 orders with each supplier

### 3.3 Sourcing Process

1. **RFI/RFP** → 20+ suppliers globally
2. **Supplier questionnaire** → shortlist 10-12
3. **Virtual/on-site audits** (or third-party reports) → 5-6 finalists
4. **Sample orders** (small batches, 50-100 units each) → test quality, lead time, communication
5. **Pricing negotiation** → target 30-40% gross margin at MSRP
6. **Contract signing** → include IP protection, quality clauses, termination terms
7. **First production run** (pilot batch of 500-1000 units per category) → QC and market test
8. **Scale production** based on sell-through data

**MOQ Strategy:**
- Initial orders: 500-1000 units per SKU (mix and match)
- Reorder MOQ: 300 units
- Build flexibility to test new SKUs with low initial runs

## 4. Business Rationale

### 4.1 Product Strategy Alignment

Our product strategy explicitly supports the brand positioning: "premium yet accessible outdoor living" with a focus on car camping and accessible glamping. The product lines are:

- **Car camping optimized:** Larger pack sizes, more comfort features, tool-free setup, colors that match your car's aesthetic
- **Glamping-ready:** Design-forward furniture, lighting, and coordinated collections that look Instagram-ready
- **Sustainability credentials:** Materials and manufacturing choices communicate eco-responsibility without performance compromise
- **Price points:** 20-50% below premium technical brands while maintaining comparable quality and better design

### 4.2 Competitive Differentiation

| Dimension | Competitors (Coleman/REI/MSR) | Our Approach |
|-----------|-------------------------------|--------------|
| Weight | Heavy (Coleman) or ultralight (MSR) | Middle ground: 20-30% lighter than mass market, "car camping light" |
| Design | Conservative (REI) or technical (MSR) | Scandinavian minimalism, neutral palette, modular coordination |
| Sustainability | Greenwashing or niche | True sustainability: recycled materials, carbon-neutral shipping, repair program |
| Price | Mass: low, Technical: very high | Mid-premium: 20-40% above mass market, 30-50% below technical |
| Ecosystem | Fragmented categories | Coordinated collections (tent + sleep system + furniture) |

### 4.3 Category Interlock

We are not just selling individual products; we are selling **complete camping lifestyles**:

- A "Weekend Warriors" bundle: tent (4P) + 2 sleeping bags + 2 pads + stove + 2 chairs + table + lighting
- A "Glamping Getaway" bundle: premium tent + deluxe sleep system + lounge chairs + lanterns + rug + side table
- A "Family Camping" bundle: 6P tent + 4 sleeping bags + family cook set + large cooler + 4 chairs

Bundling drives higher AOV and cross-selling.

### 4.4 Sourcing Advantages

By basing 70% of production in China but with Vietnam and Mexico diversification, we achieve:
- Cost competitiveness (target COGS at 35-45% of MSRP)
- Scale efficiency (amortize tooling over multiple SKUs)
- Supply chain resilience (multiple regions)
- Quality control via experienced factories and personal oversight

### 4.5 Quality & Warranty Philosophy

- **Economy tier:** 1-year limited warranty
- **Mid-Range:** 3-year limited warranty
- **Premium:** Lifetime structural warranty + 5-year fabric warranty

We will also offer:
- **Lifetime repair program:** Customers can ship broken items for repair at reasonable cost (encourages brand loyalty)
- **Trade-in & recycle:** Discount on new purchase when trading old gear (any brand); we responsibly recycle materials

### 4.6 Sustainability Commitments

- 50%+ recycled content in all polyester/nylon fabrics by Year 2
- Zero PFAS/PFC chemical treatments (use eco-friendly DWR)
- Carbon-neutral shipping (offset all inbound and outbound freight)
- Packaging: 100% recycled and recyclable materials, minimal plastic
- "Product-as-a-service": rental program for occasional users (Year 2)

## 8. Quality & Compliance

- **Certifications:** CPSC compliance (USA), CE marking (EU), REACH compliance, Prop 65 warnings where needed
- **Testing:** SGS/BV lab testing for flammability, chemical content, performance
- **Warranty terms:** Clear, easy-to-understand language; no fine print
- **Safety:** All products meet applicable safety standards; clear instructions and warnings
- **Traceability:** Batch/lot numbers for warranty and recalls

---

## 9. Sustainability & ESG

- **Materials:** Minimum 30% recycled content in all synthetic fabrics by launch (target 50% by Year 2)
- **Chemistry:** No PFAS, PFCs, or harmful dyes; bluesign® or Oeko-Tex certified where possible
- **Carbon:** Calculate and offset all shipping emissions; incentivize carbon-neutral delivery choices
- **Circularity:** Repair program; take-back and recycling; product-as-a-service trial
- **Packaging:** Minimal, recyclable, compostable where feasible
