## 1. Work Breakdown Structure (WBS)

  - 1.1.3 Product specification document
  - 1.1.4 Industrial design sketches & CAD models

- **1.2 Prototype Development**
  - 1.2.1 Alpha prototype build (3 iterations)
  - 1.2.2 Functional testing & validation
  - 1.2.3 User testing with target segment (n=20)
  - 1.2.4 Design refinements based on feedback

## 4. Risk Register & Mitigation

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation Strategy |
|------|-------------|--------|---------------------|
| **Manufacturing delays** | Medium | High | - Dual sourcing strategy<br>- Build buffer time (2 weeks)<br>- Weekly factory check-ins |
| **Cost overruns** | Medium | High | - 20% contingency budget<br>- Target cost negotiations<br>- Quarterly cost reviews |
| **Quality issues** | Low | High | - Third-party QC at factory<br>- Pilot batch approval required<br>- Clear quality specs in contract |
| **Supply chain disruption** | Low | Medium | - Identify backup suppliers<br>- Safety stock for critical components |
| **Website downtime** | Medium | Medium | - Load testing pre-launch<br>- CDN & backup hosting<br>- Monitoring & alerting |
| **Poor market reception** | Medium | High | - Pre-launch signups target 500+<br>- Beta customer testimonials<br>- A/B test landing pages |
| **IP infringement** | Low | Medium | - Trademark search before branding<br>- NDA with all suppliers<br>- Patent search for design |
| **Payment gateway issues** | Low | Medium | - Secondary gateway backup<br>- Test transactions pre-launch |
| **Team burnout** | Medium | Medium | - Sustainable pace (40-45h/wk)<br>- Clear weekend boundaries<br>- Cross-training for redundancy |
| **Competitor launch conflict** | Low | Medium | - Monitor competitor activity<br>- Differentiation in branding<br>- Flexibility in timing (±1 week) |

## 5. Meeting Cadence

### Daily Standups (15 min)
- **When**: Every weekday, 9:30 AM
- **Who**: Core team (all internal)
- **Format**:
  1. What did you complete yesterday?
  2. What will you work on today?
  3. Any blockers?
- **Tool**: Quick text update in team chat if async preferred
- **Goal**: Alignment, early problem detection

### Weekly Sync (60 min)
- **When**: Mondays, 10:00 AM
- **Who**: Full team + key external partners (invited as needed)
- **Agenda**:
  1. Wins & highlights from last week (5 min)
  2. Progress against milestones (15 min)
  3. Risk review & mitigation (10 min)
  4. Upcoming week's priorities (15 min)
  5. Cross-functional dependencies (10 min)
  6. Q&A / decisions needed (5 min)
- **Deliverable**: Meeting notes + updated project tracker

### Milestone Reviews (90 min)
- **When**: At end of each phase (Weeks 6, 9, 12)
- **Who**: Leadership + core team
- **Purpose**: Phase completion, go/no-go decisions, lessons learned
- **Deliverable**: Phase report + recommendations

### Ad-hoc Meetings
- As needed for problem-solving, design reviews, vendor negotiations
- Schedule 30-min blocks with specific agenda
- Avoid recurring meetings that aren't delivering value
